# Cross-domain compounding: the computational substrate of a Learning Society
*millisecond-era · vision + measured evidence · 2026-06 · honest scope throughout*

## One line
A computational substrate that lets **every field's verified knowledge compound — across domains, without
sliding back** — turning "他山之石" (a breakthrough in one field lifting all others) from a social metaphor
into a measurable, machine-scale engine. **Cross-domain compounding = science acceleration.**

## Three intellectual pillars (pointing at one thing)
1. **Systems science (Qian Xuesen)** — see the whole complex system *at once*, then lift it. Putting
   chemistry, genetics, medicine, biology, … into **one shared memory algebra** is the first time these
   silos sit in one observable, compoundable system.
2. **Stiglitz, *Building a Learning Society*** — societal progress comes when a breakthrough in one field is
   chased, learned, and **cross-pollinated** into others. That is exactly cross-domain compounding; here it
   has a substrate that can run it at machine scale.
3. **Determinism** — today's large models are stochastic oracles: they hallucinate, drift, can't be audited
   or accumulated, so what they "learn" slides back. A high-dimensional **bind/bundle/reverse-search**
   algebra is reversible, composable, traceable, exactly-forgettable, and **never loses** what it stored.
   A learning society needs precisely this: verified truth that *only moves forward*.

## Measured evidence (not a slide — emitted to an experiment ledger)
- **Cross-domain compounding, real chemistry + real causal biology.** Real RDKit retrosynthesis + the SACHS
  causal signalling network, bridged by real drugs: query a downstream protein → one unified memory chains
  **protein ← gene/pathway ← drug ← chemical fragments**, a full cross-domain chain. **Unified memory 1.000
  vs best single-domain 0.392 = compounding gain +0.608** (3 seeds). Neither single-domain database can
  produce the whole chain; the unified algebra can.
- **It scales to a real, open biomedical knowledge graph.** On **Hetionet** (~47k nodes / 2.25M edges, built
  from CTD + 28 public resources), a unified memory over a ~1,160-entity subgraph chains
  **Disease → Gene → Compound (+ Pathway, + regulating Gene)** across domains — **unified ≈6× the
  single-domain (disease-gene-only) memory** that stops at genes — and the full cross-domain chain recovers at
  **0.65 → 0.99** once the search is matched to the graph's real shape (robust across 4 encodings × 3 seeds at
  adequate dimension, 0.95–0.97). The 0.65 was not a representation ceiling: a gene is a 3-relation hub, and the
  recall curve's knee *measured* that hub size on its own — a worked example of the diagnostic doubling as a
  knowledge-graph measurement instrument (see `hdc/FINDING-recall-diagnostic-is-a-structure-probe.md`).
- **The memory is one engine across domains** — the same bind-and-invert algebra carries retrosynthesis,
  causal abduction, root-cause, diagnosis, recall, and analogy; only the codebook changes.
- **It reaches a genuinely distant science, and holds two at once.** The same engine carries *materials science*
  (material → constituent elements, recall **1.000**; the diagnostic's knee reads out composition complexity just
  as it read gene hub-size in biology). And **biomedicine + materials co-reside in one hypervector with zero
  cross-domain leakage** (0.000 either way) — each stays queryable, the only cost a shared-capacity tax that more
  dimension removes. One substrate, two distant sciences, losslessly separable. *(A bridged **chain** across such
  distant fields awaits a real, selective bridge dataset — flagged, not fabricated.)*
- **Capacity is linear, buyable physics** — a flat memory holds ≈ D/100 facts; sharding multiplies it; the
  routing index is broken open by free hashing. So holding all of humanity's verified knowledge is a
  linear-cost engineering bill, **not a fundamental wall.**

## Honest boundary
The algebra **does not discover** (discovery stays with experiment + large models); it **holds, composes,
traces, and never loses**. Its edge isn't "smarter" — it's *deterministic, auditable, compounding* memory.
And we hold ourselves to it: this program has caught and corrected two of its own over-claims (see
`hdc/HDC-EXPERIMENT-OPS-METHOD.md` §7 and `hdc/FINDING-recall-diagnostic-is-a-structure-probe.md`). The
rigor — every claim emitted to a ledger, walls re-tested before they're believed — is itself the moat versus
a stochastic oracle.

## Why it matters
Not "smarter AI." An engine that makes **every field's verified breakthroughs compound into every other
field**, deterministically, at machine scale — eventually on a µW edge chip anyone can own. The engine of a
Learning Society. **跨域复利 = 科学加速.**
