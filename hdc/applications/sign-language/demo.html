The Core Discovery核心发现

The popular vocabulary is the wrong vocabulary. The 25 most common signs in any dataset are NOT the 25 most distinguishable signs. Some popular sign pairs have cosine similarity 0.999 — nearly identical in feature space. Supervised contrastive learning (SupCon) only works when signs are genuinely distinct.
最常用的词汇，不是最好区分的词汇。数据集里最常见的25个手势，不是最容易被识别的25个。有些常用手势配对的余弦相似度高达0.999——在特征空间里几乎完全一样。监督对比学习（SupCon）只有在手势本身有足够差异时才能正常收敛。
