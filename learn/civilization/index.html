Featured — the five in the thesis

Owned, reversible identity

Prove "same person" without exposing the raw biometric, then surgically separate a dangerous family record — with an audit note. (Hassan, Syrian refugee.)

Dashcam evidence helper

Your car's video flags the moment someone runs a red light, builds a deterministic auditable bundle, and you choose to submit it. On-device, owned, forgettable — not auto-report.

Home / baby safety

Process an existing home camera on-device, flag an anomaly via surprise, keep only a tiny event record — and discard the raw footage. The footage never leaves home.

Factory predictive maintenance

Per-machine vibration where the alert fires on a change in harmonic structure (a failure precursor), not a fixed threshold. Baselines editable in the field. (Rita, textile plant.)

Elderly decline care

Change-only watching of a learned gait, flagging the slow drift that precedes a fall — at a power budget that lasts weeks, with no cloud video of the home. (Maria, off-grid.)
