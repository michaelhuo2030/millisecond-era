The Power of Speed ⚡速度的力量 ⚡

The AI you use every day runs at about 50 chars/sec—human reading speed. But when an AI can think thousands to hundreds of thousands of steps per second, what it unlocks isn't "answering faster"—it's a whole new class of things you couldn't do before: exploring a thousand futures at once, letting a whole society evolve in real time, sub-millisecond reflexes.
The first one (🔥 Real WebGPU) is a real model—a tiny real model generating right on your GPU, showing real tok/s (downloads once the first time). The other 7 are pure front-end simulations (running templates/HDC in your browser, zero download, zero network), built to show "what speed unlocks."
In the time it takes to type one sentence, watch a whole history of civilization.你每天用的 AI 大约 50 字/秒——人类阅读速度。但当一个 AI 每秒能想几千到几十万步时,解锁的不是"答得更快",而是一整类全新的事:同时探索上千个未来、让一整个社会实时演化、亚毫秒级反射。
下面 第一个(🔥 真·WebGPU)是真模型——一个极小的真模型在你的 GPU 上当场生成、显示真实 tok/s(首次下载一次)。其余 7 个是纯前端模拟(用模板/HDC 在浏览器里跑,零下载、零联网),专门演示"速度解锁了什么"。

Which are real, which are simulated?
• 🔥 Real WebGPU = a real model running for real, on your browser's GPU, showing your device's real tok/s (downloads a small model once the first time).
• The other 7 = pure simulations (templates/HDC): the content is simulated, but the speed numbers shown inside are really measured; used to demo "what speed unlocks."
• There are also a few versions using bigger real local models (real Qwen generating on the spot, full power, camera video)—these need you to download the model + start a local server on your own computer, can't be hosted as a static web page, so they live in the project repo, run locally per the instructions.哪些真、哪些模拟?
• 🔥 真·WebGPU = 真模型真跑,在你浏览器的 GPU 上,显示你设备的真实 tok/s(首次下载一次小模型)。
• 其余 7 个 = 纯模拟(模板/HDC):内容是模拟的,但里面展示的速度数字是真实测得的;用来演示"速度解锁了什么"。
• 还有几个用更大的真本地模型的版本(真 Qwen 当场生成、火力全开、摄像头视频)——要在你自己电脑上下载模型 + 起本地服务器才能跑,没法挂静态网页,放在 项目仓库里按说明本地运行。
These are software + small-model/HDC demos, proving "what speed unlocks"—not chip speed. The chip's job: move these capabilities onto bigger models and societies, and cut the power down to what an edge device / battery can handle.
